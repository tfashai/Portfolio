The Problem

Your inbox is losing you deals

You work hard generating leads on social media. But if you can't respond in minutes, those leads go cold — or go to a competitor who can.

01

Slow replies kill momentum

A buyer sees your listing, gets excited, sends a DM. You see it three hours later. By then they've toured a property from an agent who responded in seconds. That deal is gone.

02

Same questions, every single day

"Is this still available?" "What's the price?" "Can I schedule a viewing?" You type the same answers dozens of times a week — time you should spend closing deals.

Most buyers browse listings in the evening. They DM you at 9pm. You see it at 8am. That gap is all it takes to lose a serious buyer to an agent who was available.
