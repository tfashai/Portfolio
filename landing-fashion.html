Who is ÀṢÀ

ÀṢÀ is a Lagos-based slow fashion brand built on the principle that African fashion deserves to be worn intentionally, not disposably. Every collection is limited by design — not as a marketing trick, but because each piece is made by hand by a small team of craftspeople in Lagos and Abeokuta. We've shipped to 28 countries. We've never done a second run of a sold-out piece.
